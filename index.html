Sandwich Corner

The L.Abines Sandwich Corner

Nestled within the bustling school campus, Sandwich Corner was founded by Lito Abines in 2013 and holds a special place in the hearts of both students and staff. It has gained a reputation for its scrumptious sandwiches, welcoming service, affordable prices, and cozy atmosphere, making it a favorite spot for satisfying our culinary cravings amidst the academic hustle and bustle.

Sandwich Corner is a kiosk type that serves fresh and healthy sandwiches. Strategically located in Bloemen Hall De La Salle University.Sandwich Corner quickly become the snacks destination inside the school campus, serving faculties, employees, and students. Sandwich Corner usually attracs 35% new customers every year.

Sandwich Corner has identified three keys that will be instrumental in its success. First is the design and implementation of strict financial controls, which will be important, since the kiosk/food industry is quite competitive. The second is that it offers high-quality fresh and affordable food to clearly stand out from the competition. The last key is the need to ensure proper visibility. Sandwich Corner must have an effective, targeted marketing campaign to support the opening of the store in order to ensure enough business.
