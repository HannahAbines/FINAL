About The Business

Lito B. Abines

Owner



Sandwich Corner was founded by Lito B. Abines. He was born in Tacloban City, he is an experienced person on the food and beverage industry. Wherein worked for Zaide Cafe Restaurant for seventeen (17) years before moving on to go into his own business.


Sandwich Corner is a small business kiosk that has been serving delicious sandwiches since its founding in 2013. Situated inside a bustling campus, Lito's stall has become a go-to spot for students and staff alike. With a passion for creating mouthwatering culinary creations, Lito consistently impresses customers with his innovative sandwich recipes and fresh ingredients sourced from local suppliers. Beyond his culinary skills, Lito is known for his warm and friendly personality, making every visit to Sandwich Corner a delightful experience. Through his dedication and entrepreneurial spirit, Lito has successfully established a beloved food destination within the campus community.

It's not just about serving delicious food to you. It's all about creating a connection, which is why we get down with the devise meals that are both fresh and flavorful.

The customer trust is our main service!
